DeMorgan's Theorem

In Boolean algebra and digital circuit design, DeMorgan's Theorem provides mathematical laws that allow us to convert mathematical conjunctions (AND) into disjunctions (OR) and vice-versa using inversion (NOT).

This has huge real-world implications: it proves you can build any digital circuit using only NAND gates or only NOR gates, which dramatically streamlines hardware manufacturing pipelines.

The Two Mathematical Laws

  • The complement of a product is equal to the sum of the complements.
    A • B = A + B
  • The complement of a sum is equal to the product of the complements.
    A + B = A • B

The Mnemonic Shortcut: "Break the line and change the sign."
